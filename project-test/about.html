About Musical Forecast

A web application developed with HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript, which generates a custom playlist for the user based on the current weather conditions of the location entered by the user. The playlists will dynamically change based upon the user’s hometown or their dream getaway location. The application’s algorithm will factor in temperature, precipitation, humidity, amount of sunshine, etc.
