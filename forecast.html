About the app

Musical Forecast is a creative application brought to you by MHKJ, which combines weather and music into a powerful application. Musical Forecast is consistently being updated to provide you with up to date weather changes, along with music to match the moment. Enjoy the rhythm of the night, or dance on with the sunshine, or perhaps you like to sing in the rain. Whatever you enjoy or have planned you will be prepared with Musical Forecast who will always have you prepared for the day as well as the forecast for the next 5 days. Thank you and enjoy the Musical Forecast.

About us
